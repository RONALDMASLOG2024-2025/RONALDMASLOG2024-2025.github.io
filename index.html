Projects

Check Int: Daily Time Record System

This automated system streamlines employee timekeeping by automatically recording clock-in and clock-out times. It eliminates manual processes and ensures accurate time tracking, leading to improved efficiency and payroll accuracy.

Online Enrollment System

As a senior high school student, I developed an online enrollment system to streamline the enrollment process. This innovative tool allows students to conveniently register for classes, view their schedules, and access important information from the comfort of their own homes. By automating many of the administrative tasks involved in enrollment, this system saves time and effort for both students and school staff.  

Bein Savis: UIC Borrowing System

This is an ongoing project for our SIA that aims to create a system for the University of Immaculate Conception (UIC) to manage borrowing and returning of library materials. The system will streamline the borrowing process, improve record-keeping, and enhance overall library efficiency.
